About Greeners Mate

Greeners Mate is a mobile application designed to help farmers, vendors, and partners in Andhra & Telangana track profits, expenditures, crop-wise earnings, and dues. Built with voice assistance and Telugu language support, it empowers rural communities with digital tools for easy recordkeeping.

Our Mission

To digitally empower large-scale farmers of Andhra & Telangana by simplifying their financial and crop management through a user-friendly mobile application.

  • Track profit, expenditures, and dues partner-wise
  • Input data through speech (supports Telugu)
  • Analyze historical crop performance
  • Enable farmers to shift from paper to mobile
